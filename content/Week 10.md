# Reflection


This week I made the tough decision to abandon pursuing a Pharos project to be played at the Capitol Theatre. Following my milestone presentation and the feedback I received on my other projects, it will be a better use of my time to focus on developing the other 2 projects.

Playing the Sublime video project I made, the thought occurred that to improve the the impact of the sublime affect, implementing an audio reactive component to the video may greatly improve it. While the void that I generated was decent work around for a lack of VFX or animation skills, it might be a good idea to explore Touch Designer as some of my fellow students have done.
