# Reflection

Overall, I am genuinely pleased with the progression and outcomes of the two main projects I submitted this semester. The experience has been incredibly rewarding, and I feel a strong sense of eagerness to continue developing these works, treating feedback not as a critique, but as a valuable roadmap for improvement. That being said, this semester presented several powerful learning experiences that I will carry forward. I will certainly regret the failure to fully make use of the opportunity to create a work specifically for the Capitol Theatre. This was a failure that highlights the importance of seizing the opportunities I am able to have during my studies. Looking ahead, I am excited to continue growing the skills I have been cultivating especially in the realm of sound design. What began as an area of study is quickly blossoming into a passionate hobby, and I have already started to pursue collaborations and personal projects outside of my studies, cementing sound design as a potentially significant and ongoing part of my creative life.
