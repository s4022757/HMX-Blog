# HME

The Heightened Multisensory Experience adjective that most appeals to me is:

## ***Sublime***


# Example works

## 1. X-Verse - Ryoji Ikeda
*Installation - Audio Visual*
***
Presents pure data as a visual and sonic experience. The audience is overwhelmed by fast-moving numeric streams, flickering lights, and noise which all evoke the sublime through abstraction, scale, and the digital unknown.

Link to artist's [website](https://www.ryojiikeda.com/project/x_verse/).
## 2. Polymorphism Data centre Simulation - Kynan Tan
*Video - 3D Model & Sound*
***
Reveals the hidden physicality of data by depicting a simulated data centre, rendering visible the vast, concealed infrastructures behind everyday digital life. The deluge of information only revealed through the flicker of diodes and the disorientating whir of spinning components, energy devoured. The work transforms abstract computation into a haunting, industrial sublime that's monolithic, inaccessible, and *here*, loudly omnipresent.

Link to artist's [website](https://kynantan.com/polymorphism/).
## 3. Everything Everywhere All at Once - D Kwan & D Scheinert
*Film*
***
The film renders the infinite chaos of the multiverse into a driving emotional experience suggesting while too vast to comprehend, it is yet deeply human. It overwhelms with scale and fragmentation, then pierces through with intimate, transcendent clarity. Its sublime lies in confronting meaninglessness not with despair, but with with a sincere and tender love of the absurdity and reckoning with the chaos of an always-connected world.

## 4. ZEE - Kurt Hentschlager
*Installation - Multisensory*
***
ZEE immerses visitors in a dense fog lit by pulsing stroboscopic light, dissolving space, time, and self into a luminous, constantly shifting void. The work overwhelms perceptions to momentarily challenge audience awareness, provoking awe, fear, and transcendence through sensory intensity. By exposing the audience to a vast psychedelic unknown, the work creates a sublime experience of disorientation that opens toward infinity.

Link to artist's [website](https://kurthentschlager.com/ZEE-2008).
## 5. The Weather Project - Olafur Eliasson
*Installation - Multisensory*
***

An indoor physical simulation of the sun using monofrequency lamps and mist installed within Tate Modern’s Turbine Hall. The scale and sensory immersion create an artificial but emotionally powerful natural phenomenon which blurs together a digital, sensory, and architectural experience.

Link to artist's [website](https://olafureliasson.net/artwork/the-weather-project-2003/).
## 6. Quantum Memories - Refik Anadol
*Installation - Multisensory - AI generated image*
***
**Quantum Memories** presents an constantly shifting visual landscape generated by translating raw data of the real through quantum algorithms, immersing the audience in a speculative parallel reality shaped by machine learning and chaotic randomness. Its sublime arises from the encounter with the incomprehensible scale and profound beauty of the parallel worlds of the machine eye which renders unseen possibility into aesthetic forms.

Link to artist's [website](https://refikanadol.com/works/quantummemories/).
## 7. TeamLab Borderless
*Immersive digital art museum*
***
These rooms overflow with responsive projections that extend infinitely through mirrored surfaces. Viewers lose spatial boundaries, immersed in shifting algorithms and endless digital patterns. The entire gallery and its borderless worlds create tech driven awe through generative beauty.

Link to gallery's websites [current](https://www.teamlab.art/e/tokyo/) & [past](https://www.teamlab.art/e/borderless-odaiba/).
## 8. Within Without - James Turrell 
*Installation - Architecture*
***
**Within Without** is a meditative sanctuary where atmosphere, light, and architecture converge. Its sublime emerges in the collapse of immensity into intimacy, framing the sky as both boundless and near. By slowing time, providing stillness, and amplifying the presence of the audience, the work evokes a subtle awe and an encounter with the vast and unknowable.

Link to artist's [website](https://rodencrater.com/about/).
## 9. Sagrada Familia - Gaudi
*Architecture*
***
A fusion of natural form, religious symbolism, and complex geometry. The interior feels both earthly and celestial, a kaleidoscope of colour, form and height, which shrinks the viewer in its grandeur and invites contemplation. The sublime arises through the tension between intimate and the infinite, human and the divine.

Link to building's [website](https://sagradafamilia.org/en/history-of-the-temple).
## 10. Incoming / The Enclave - Richard Mosse
*Films*
***
Using military-grade surveillance tech such as IR and thermal imaging, Richard Mosse's work renders the trauma of humanitarian crises and conflict in surreal and harrowing images, transforming scenes of violence into dreamlike landscapes and evoking moral and existential unease. The sublime emerges through the tension, where beauty and horror collapse into a single, haunting vision beyond moral or emotional clarity.

*Incoming* NGV exhibition [website](https://www.ngv.vic.gov.au/multimedia/richard-mosse/).
*The Enclave* NGV exhibition [website](https://www.ngv.vic.gov.au/exhibition/richard-mosse/).
## 11. Monument Valley - Ustwo
*Game Series*
***
A digital maze of impossible geometry, echoing Escher and sacred architecture. The game uses calm music, vast spatial illusions, and architectural impossibility to inspire emotional elevation and serene wonder.

Link to game's [website](https://www.monumentvalleygame.com/mv3).
